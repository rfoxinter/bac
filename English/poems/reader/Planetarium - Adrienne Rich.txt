<p class="poem">     <i>Thinking of Caroline Herschel (1750–1848)</i></p>
<p class="poem">     <i>astronomer, sister of William; and others.</i></p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">A woman in the shape of a monster</p>
<p class="poem">a monster in the shape of a woman</p>
<p class="poem">the skies are full of them</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">a woman      ‘in the snow</p>
<p class="poem">among the Clocks and instruments</p>
<p class="poem">or measuring the ground with poles’</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">in her 98 years to discover</p>
<p class="poem">8 comets</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">she whom the moon ruled</p>
<p class="poem">like us</p>
<p class="poem">levitating into the night sky</p>
<p class="poem">riding the polished lenses</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Galaxies of women, there</p>
<p class="poem">doing penance for impetuousness</p>
<p class="poem">ribs chilled</p>
<p class="poem">in those spaces    of the mind</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">An eye,</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">          ‘virile, precise and absolutely certain’</p>
<p class="poem">          from the mad webs of Uranusborg</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">                                                            encountering the NOVA</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">every impulse of light exploding</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">from the core</p>
<p class="poem">as life flies out of us</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">             Tycho whispering at last</p>
<p class="poem">             ‘Let me not seem to have lived in vain’</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">What we see, we see</p>
<p class="poem">and seeing is changing</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">the light that shrivels a mountain</p>
<p class="poem">and leaves a man alive</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Heartbeat of the pulsar</p>
<p class="poem">heart sweating through my body</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">The radio impulse</p>
<p class="poem">pouring in from Taurus</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">         I am bombarded yet         I stand</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">I have been standing all my life in the</p>
<p class="poem">direct path of a battery of signals</p>
<p class="poem">the most accurately transmitted most</p>
<p class="poem">untranslatable language in the universe</p>
<p class="poem">I am a galactic cloud so deep      so invo-</p>
<p class="poem">luted that a light wave could take 15</p>
<p class="poem">years to travel through me       And has</p>
<p class="poem">taken      I am an instrument in the shape</p>
<p class="poem">of a woman trying to translate pulsations</p>
<p class="poem">into images    for the relief of the body</p>
<p class="poem">and the reconstruction of the mind.</p>
