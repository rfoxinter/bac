<p class="poem">Stasis in darkness.</p>
<p class="poem">Then the substanceless blue</p>
<p class="poem">Pour of tor and distances.</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">God’s lioness,</p>
<p class="poem">How one we grow,</p>
<p class="poem">Pivot of heels and knees!—The furrow</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Splits and passes, sister to</p>
<p class="poem">The brown arc</p>
<p class="poem">Of the neck I cannot catch,</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Nigger-eye</p>
<p class="poem">Berries cast dark</p>
<p class="poem">Hooks—</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Black sweet blood mouthfuls,</p>
<p class="poem">Shadows.</p>
<p class="poem">Something else</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Hauls me through air—</p>
<p class="poem">Thighs, hair;</p>
<p class="poem">Flakes from my heels.</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">White</p>
<p class="poem">Godiva, I unpeel—</p>
<p class="poem">Dead hands, dead stringencies.</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">And now I</p>
<p class="poem">Foam to wheat, a glitter of seas.</p>
<p class="poem">The child’s cry</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Melts in the wall.</p>
<p class="poem">And I</p>
<p class="poem">Am the arrow,</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">The dew that flies</p>
<p class="poem">Suicidal, at one with the drive</p>
<p class="poem">Into the red</p>
<br class="poem"/>
<p class="poem">Eye, the cauldron of morning.</p>
